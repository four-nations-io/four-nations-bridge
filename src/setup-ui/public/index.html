    About this bridge

    This bridge indexes your content folder and serves thumbnails + previews to your account over an outbound encrypted connection. Your content stays on this machine — the service never stores your full files.

    Your account is paired with this bridge — sign in from any device and your content library is available there. To re-pair (new pairing code or a different account), see the “Re-pairing” section of the install docs: delete paired.json from the bridge’s state folder and restart the container.

    Failing handshake? Check the pairing is intact (this page shows “Pairing: wizard” or “Pairing: env”) and that CONTENT_BRIDGE_SAAS_URL resolves from this container’s network namespace.
